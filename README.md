# Business-Intelligence-Dashboard-for-Sales-Insights---Power-BI

##  Overview
This project features a comprehensive multi-page Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, regional growth, and product trends for a global retail/manufacturing entity. The dashboard provides actionable insights through three specialized views: **Executive Summary**, **Trend & Growth Analysis**, and **Product Performance**.

---

##  Dashboard Pages & Insights

### 1. Executive Summary
Provides a high-level overview of the business health.
* **KPI Cards:** Tracking Total Due (**$1.33bn**), TAX Amount, Total Revenue, and Total Freight.
* **Order Trends:** Analysis of monthly order volume, highlighting peak periods.
* **Geographical Analysis:** Global revenue distribution with focus on North America and Australia.

### 2. Trend & Growth Analysis
Focuses on temporal changes and financial efficiency.
* **Growth Tracking:** Monitoring MoM Growth and Revenue vs. Goals (+166% achievement).
* **Waterfall Chart:** Visualizing monthly revenue changes and cumulative growth.
* **Freight Efficiency:** Tracking the Freight Ratio % to optimize shipping costs.

### 3. Product Performance
Deep dive into product-level metrics and logistics.
* **Product Hierarchy:** Revenue breakdown by Category (Components, Bikes, Clothing).
* **Top Products:** Identification of high-revenue items like the "Mountain 200" series.
* **Logistics:** Analysis of shipping methods (87.9% via Truck Ground).

---

##  Tools & Technologies
* **Power BI Desktop:** Visualization and Dashboard design.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Calculated measures for MoM Growth, Sales YTD, and Freight Ratios.
* **Power Query:** Data cleaning, transformation, and ETL.
* **Data Modeling:** Implementation of a Star Schema for optimized reporting.
* **M Language:** Date Dim

---

##  Key Insights Derived
* **Revenue Driver:** The **Components** category is the primary revenue leader ($43.91M).
* **Seasonal Trends:** Significant upward trend in orders starting from July, peaking in December.
* **Market Leader:** **Australia** stands out as the top-performing territory in terms of order volume.
